Formula FPV London

September 13, 2015

Race format:

Three rounds of qualifying, 3 laps in 3 minutes, points awarded for speed and distance.
Qualifying positions calculated from best two scores and used to seed the tournament bracket.
Grand Final run over 4 laps in 4 minutes.
